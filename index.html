Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. Under his leadership, the Tata Group became a global powerhouse. Despite his success, he’s known for being humble, ethical, and deeply committed to social causes and innovation.

Why I Admire Him

  • He expanded Tata Group globally — acquiring Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley Tea while keeping Indian values intact.
  • He always emphasized purpose over profit, investing in education, healthcare, and rural development.
  • His calm leadership and humility make him a role model for ethical entrepreneurship and responsible capitalism.
